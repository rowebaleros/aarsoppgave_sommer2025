How to Play Tic Tac Toe

Objective

The goal of Tic Tac Toe is to be the first player to get three of your marks (X or O) in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

How to Play

  1. The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  2. Player X always goes first.
  3. Players take turns placing their mark (X or O) in an empty square.
  4. The first player to get three marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins the game.
  5. If all nine squares are filled and no player has three in a row, the game ends in a draw.

Tips

  • Try to block your opponent from getting three in a row while working on your own strategy.
  • Control the center square for more chances to win.
